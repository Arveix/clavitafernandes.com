#modernlovestorycampaign

Through its Modern Love series campaign, Amazon Prime India urged Mumbai residents to share their #modernlovestory on Twitter, resulting in around 88 billboards painted across the city with local love stories. Along with the team at Socheers, helped design for 60 design adapts out of the 88 for the billboards.
